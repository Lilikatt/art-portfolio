Fantasy RPG

Role: Lead Concept Artist

Year: 2024

Platform: PC, Console

Team Size: 12 people

Project Overview

This fantasy RPG required a rich, immersive world filled with diverse characters and environments. I was responsible for establishing the visual style and creating key concept art that would guide the entire art team.

My Contributions

  • Character design for 15+ main characters
  • Environment concepts for 8 major locations
  • Visual development and style guide creation
  • Collaboration with 3D artists for asset creation

Challenges & Solutions

The main challenge was creating a cohesive fantasy world that felt both familiar and unique. I developed a distinctive art style that blended realistic proportions with stylized lighting and color palettes.

Photoshop, Blender, Procreate
